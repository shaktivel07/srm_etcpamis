About SRM Institute of Science and Technology: 
SRM Institute of Science and Technology is one of India's leading multidisciplinary higher education institutions, recognized for its excellence in teaching, research, innovation, and industry engagement. Established with a vision to impart quality education and foster technological advancement, SRMIST offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate, postgraduate, doctoral, and post-doctoral programs across diverse disciplines, including Engineering and Technology, Management, Medicine and Health Sciences, Science and Humanities, Law, Agricultural Sciences, and Allied Health Sciences. In NIRF 2025, SRMIST achieved the 22nd rank in the Overall category and 11th among universities in India, demonstrating its strong commitment to quality higher education and research excellence.

The institution has a strong academic ecosystem supported by highly qualified faculty members, state-of-the-art laboratories, modern research facilities, innovation centers, and extensive industry collaborations. SRMIST continuously promotes interdisciplinary research and entrepreneurship through partnerships with leading universities, research organizations, and industries across the globe. The university attracts students from all over India and numerous countries, creating a vibrant multicultural learning environment. With campuses located in Kattankulathur (Chennai), Ramapuram, Vadapalani, Tiruchirappalli, Modinagar, Sonepat, Amaravati, and Gangtok, SRMIST has established itself as a center of excellence for higher education and research. The institution consistently emphasizes outcome-based education, innovation-driven learning, global exposure, and societal impact, preparing graduates to become competent professionals, researchers, and leaders in their respective domains.

About the Department of Computer Science and Engineering:
The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Tiruchirappalli, was established in 2021 with the vision of imparting quality education and fostering innovation in the field of computing. The department is committed to developing competent professionals through a curriculum that combines strong theoretical foundations with practical and industry-oriented learning. The department offers a vibrant academic environment supported by experienced faculty members, modern computing laboratories, advanced software tools, and state-of-the-art infrastructure. It actively promotes research and innovation in emerging areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, and the Internet of Things.

To bridge the gap between academia and industry, the department collaborates with leading organizations through internships, workshops, expert talks, certification programs, and industry-driven projects. Students are encouraged to participate in research activities, hackathons, technical competitions, and entrepreneurial initiatives. The department strives to nurture technically skilled, ethically responsible, and globally competent graduates capable of addressing real-world challenges and contributing to technological advancement. Objective of the Conference:
The International Conference on Emerging Trends in Computational Physics, Applied Mathematics and Intelligent Systems (ETCPAMIS 2027) aims to provide a premier interdisciplinary platform for academicians, researchers, scientists, industry professionals, and students to present and discuss recent advancements, innovative methodologies, and emerging research trends in Computational Physics, Applied Mathematics, Scientific Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and Intelligent Systems.The conference seeks to foster collaboration among academia, research institutions, and industries by encouraging the exchange of novel ideas, scientific discoveries, computational techniques, and technological innovations that address real-world scientific and engineering challenges.
The conference further aims to promote high-quality research in areas such as mathematical modelling, computational simulations, quantum computing, physics-informed artificial intelligence, intelligent automation, scientific data analytics, sustainable technologies, and interdisciplinary intelligent systems. Through keynote lectures, technical sessions, and scholarly interactions, ETCPAMIS 2027 aspires to contribute significantly to scientific advancement, technological innovation, and global research collaboration in emerging computational and intelligent technologies.

Conference Scope: 
The conference will focus on the emerging trends and interdisciplinary applications of Computational Physics, Applied Mathematics, Scientific Computing, and Intelligent Systems in addressing complex scientific, engineering, and technological challenges. It aims to provide a platform for researchers, scientists, academicians, and industry professionals to explore innovative computational models, mathematical frameworks, intelligent algorithms, and emerging technologies that accelerate scientific discovery and technological advancement. The conference serves as a premier interdisciplinary forum for presenting and discussing cutting-edge research, innovative methodologies, and practical applications in Computational Physics, Applied Mathematics, Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, Scientific Simulations, and Intelligent Systems. The conference seeks to foster collaboration among academia, industry, and research organizations to address contemporary scientific and engineering problems while promoting sustainable and intelligent technological solutions for the benefit of society.

Review Process: 
a) At first, the manuscripts received would be evaluated from the purview of the scope of this conference.
b) Screened submissions undergo an initial quality check through Turnitin Software for potential similarity with published literature.
c) Subsequently, the manuscripts with a similarity index of less than 5 percent would be sent for single-blind review by three independent referees
d) A consensus of three reviewers would be mandatory for the acceptance of manuscripts
e) One reviewer would not review more than three manuscripts

Publication and Indexing: The articles submitted to the International Conference on Generative AI Applications in Science and Technology will be subjected to double-blind peer review by the panel of experts. All accepted and registered papers will be included in the conference proceedings that will be published by AIP. (Waiting for approval)

Best Paper Award: One paper from each of the listed tracks will be selected as Best Paper. The Best Papers will be announced at culmination of the conference.
